Wind Speed

This wind speed plot reveals that there were very high winds in polar regions ( high latitudes) on the date the data were collected. Otherwise wind speed doesn't appear to have a strong dependence on latitude. An analysis over many days/weeks/months would be necessary to make any strong statements about the latitudinal dependence of wind speed, since wind speeds can fluctuately quickly.
